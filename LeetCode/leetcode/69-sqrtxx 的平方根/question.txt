sqrtx
实现<code>int sqrt(int x)</code>函数。

计算并返回<em>x</em>的平方根，其中<em>x </em>是非负整数。

由于返回类型是整数，结果只保留整数的部分，小数部分将被舍去。

<strong>示例 1:</strong>

<pre><strong>输入:</strong> 4
<strong>输出:</strong> 2
</pre>

<strong>示例 2:</strong>

<pre><strong>输入:</strong> 8
<strong>输出:</strong> 2
<strong>说明:</strong> 8 的平方根是 2.82842..., 
    由于返回类型是整数，小数部分将被舍去。
</pre>

x 的平方根
Implement <code>int sqrt(int x)</code>.

Compute and return the square root of <em>x</em>, where<em>x</em>is guaranteed to be a non-negative integer.

Since the return typeis an integer, the decimal digits are truncated and only the integer part of the resultis returned.

<strong>Example 1:</strong>

<pre>
<strong>Input:</strong> 4
<strong>Output:</strong> 2
</pre>

<strong>Example 2:</strong>

<pre>
<strong>Input:</strong> 8
<strong>Output:</strong> 2
<strong>Explanation:</strong> The square root of 8 is 2.82842..., and since 
            the decimal part is truncated, 2 is returned.
</pre>
