### 解题思路
    非常简单的分组排序就行了

### 代码

```oraclesql
SELECT D.NAME DEPARTMENT, E.NAME EMPLOYEE, E.SALARY
  FROM DEPARTMENT D,
       (SELECT E.*,
               DENSE_RANK() OVER(PARTITION BY E.DEPARTMENTID ORDER BY E.SALARY DESC) RK
          FROM EMPLOYEE E) E
 WHERE D.ID = E.DEPARTMENTID(+)
   AND E.RK <= 3
```
