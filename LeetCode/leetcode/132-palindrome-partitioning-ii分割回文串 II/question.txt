palindrome-partitioning-ii
给定一个字符串 <em>s</em>，将 <em>s</em> 分割成一些子串，使每个子串都是回文串。

返回符合要求的最少分割次数。

<strong>示例:</strong>

<pre><strong>输入:</strong>&quot;aab&quot;
<strong>输出:</strong> 1
<strong>解释: </strong>进行一次分割就可将<em>s </em>分割成 [&quot;aa&quot;,&quot;b&quot;] 这样两个回文子串。
</pre>

分割回文串 II
Given a string <em>s</em>, partition <em>s</em> such that every substring of the partition is a palindrome.

Return the minimum cuts needed for a palindrome partitioning of <em>s</em>.

<strong>Example:</strong>

<pre>
<strong>Input:</strong>&quot;aab&quot;
<strong>Output:</strong> 1
<strong>Explanation:</strong> The palindrome partitioning [&quot;aa&quot;,&quot;b&quot;] could be produced using 1 cut.
</pre>
