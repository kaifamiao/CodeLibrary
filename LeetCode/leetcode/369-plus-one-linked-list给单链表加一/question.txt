plus-one-linked-list
用一个 <strong>非空 </strong>单链表来表示一个非负整数，然后将这个整数加一。

你可以假设这个整数除了 0 本身，没有任何前导的 0。

这个整数的各个数位按照 <strong>高位在链表头部</strong>、<strong>低位在链表尾部</strong>的顺序排列。

<strong>示例:</strong>

<pre><strong>输入: </strong>[1,2,3]
<strong>输出: </strong>[1,2,4]
</pre>

给单链表加一
Given a non-negative integer represented as <b>non-empty</b> a singly linked list of digits, plus one to the integer.

You may assume the integer do not contain any leading zero, except the number 0 itself.

The digits are stored such that the most significant digit is at the head of the list.

<div>
<strong>Example :</strong>

<pre>
<strong>Input: </strong><span id="example-input-1-1">[1,2,3]</span>
<strong>Output: </strong><span id="example-output-1">[1,2,4]</span>
</pre>
</div>
